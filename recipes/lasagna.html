Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a popular Italian dish enjoyed for lunch or dinner.

It is simple to make and consists of layers stacked on top of one another

Ingredients:

  • meat - typically Italian sausage and/or ground beef
  • onion and garlic
  • crushed tomatoes, 2 tomato sauce cans, and 2 tomato paste cans
  • lasagna noodles
  • cheeses - mozzarella, parmesan, and ricotta
